API History

Un ejemplo del uso de la API History

Click the "Back" button in the browser to go back one page in the history.

Click the "Forward" button in the browser to go forward one page in the history.

Click the "Go 3 pages forward" button to go forward three pages in the history.

Click the "Go 2 pages back" button to go back two pages in the history.

Click the "Go to homepage" button to go to the homepage.
